## Final prompt set

Both prompts requested polished hand-drawn anime game-sprite illustrations in
an exact borderless 3×2 grid on a uniform `#00ff00` chroma-key field, with one
complete full-body pose per cell, generous padding, opaque effects, and no
opponent, scenery, labels, borders, watermark, shadow, gradient, floor, blood,
or gore.

Blueno-fight Luffy pose order: rooftop guard; Gear Second activation; Jet
Pistol; Jet Bazooka; steam overdrive; victory hold. Blueno pose order: Iron
Body guard; Door-Door evasion; Air Door ambush; Iron Body impact; Jet Bazooka
defeat; seated defeat.

Sanji pose order: kick-ready guard; Collier-style high kick; spinning counter;
first Diable Jambe ignition; Flambage Shot; smoking victory. Jabra pose order:
wolf-hybrid guard; Wolf Fang rush; Iron Body Kenpo; claw counter; flaming-kick
impact; seated defeat. Sanji's first three poses were required to remain
flame-free so the ignition reads as an authored transformation rather than a
permanent costume effect.

Zoro pose order: Three-Sword Style guard; Tempest Kick counter; tower clash;
Asura manifestation; Nine-Sword Asura dash; sheathed victory. Kaku pose order:
giraffe-hybrid Four-Sword guard; four-sword spin; Tempest Kick; compressed
Pasta Machine charge; Asura impact; collapsed defeat. Zoro used a magenta key
to preserve his green hair and sash; the keyed sheet was regenerated without
magenta despill after visual review caught an unwanted skin-color shift.

Lucci-fight Luffy pose order: Gear Second guard with compact opaque steam; Jet Pistol;
Jet Whip; Gear Third giant-arm windup; Jet Gatling with both arms driving
forward and many distinct fist afterimages; exhausted kneeling victory. The
Gatling cell was explicitly required to read as a barrage rather than one
stretched punch.

Lucci pose order: leopard-hybrid guard; Soru rush; Finger Pistol; Tempest Kick;
Rokuogan two-palm shockwave; overwhelmed Jet Gatling defeat. Identity lock:
spotted golden fur, black mane-like hair, feline face and ears, tail, dark
formal trousers and suspenders.

The green backgrounds, and Zoro's magenta background, were removed locally with the installed imagegen
chroma-key helper using border auto-key, soft matte, threshold 12/220, and
despill. Zoro intentionally omits despill to protect skin color. All eight
keyed sheets were visually reviewed before atlas construction.

Before public or commercial distribution, obtain a separate rights review.
Technical readiness is not a claim of franchise permission.
